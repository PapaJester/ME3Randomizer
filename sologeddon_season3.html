ScrubaGeddon will consist of 3 different phases, each tied to the difficulty the matches will be played on. The first of these phases will be The Bronze Age.

The entire season will be played using brand new accounts, which will have all characters unlocked, and 255 Gels, Missiles, Ops Packs, and Thermal Clips added by means of VERY ADVANCED HACKING. The accounts will start with nothing else that isn't given to them by the game. In addition, no player may open the Gift Packs provided in the store until the first time they get an extraction in a solo.

Unlike previous seasons, Perks will no longer be earned in the course of ScrubaGeddon. Rather, they may be earned as a prize for winning a SoloGeddon One-Shot. The Perk to be awarded to the winner will be declared before the One-Shot is started.

At any point during ScrubaGeddon, a Player may elect to Promote a Class in exchange for a bounty of 300,000 in-game credits.

Each Phase of Season 3 will start with a duo, and the Graduation match will be a solo with special conditions. A prize will be awarded to the player with the most wins in a Phase.
